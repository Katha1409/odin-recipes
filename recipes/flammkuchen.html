Flammkuchen

Description

The best food there is!

Ingredients

  • 200g Flour
  • 1Tsp Salt
  • 1Tbsp Oil
  • 110ml Water
  • 200g Sour cream
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Nutmeg
  • 2 Onions
  • 100g Bacon bits or strips
  • 50g Grated cheese

Steps

  1. Combine Flour, Salt, Oil and Water. Knead dough until it no longer sticks to your hands, otherwise add a little more flour
  2. Let dough rest for 30 Minutes
  3. Stir sour cream until smooth and season with salt, pepper and nutmeg
  4. Preheat oven to 200Â°C (top/bottom heat).
  5. Roll out dough thinly with a rolling pin one a flat surface and place it on a backing tray. Spread with sour cream and scatter the onion, cheese and bacon.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes.
