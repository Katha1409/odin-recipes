Nutella Brownie Cookies

Description

I love Cookies!!

Ingredients

Nutella dough:
  • 150g Dark chocolate
  • 40g Unsalted butter
  • 1 Egg
  • 55g Brown Sugar
  • 1 pinch of Salt
  • 50g Nutella
  • 3g Vanilla extract
  • 60g Plain flour
  • 2g Baking powder
  • 1g Baking soda
White dough:
  • 75g Unsalted butter
  • 55g Sugar
  • 1 pinch of Salt
  • 25g Egg
  • 2g Vanilla extract
  • 150g Cake flour

Steps

Nutella dough:
  1. Microwave dark chocolate and butter for about 30 seconds.
  2. Combine egg, brown sugar and salt. Then add the microwaved mixture. Mix in Nutella and vanilla extract
  3. Sift and mix in the flour, baking powder and baking soda. Refrigerate mixture for about 1 hour.
White dough:
  1. Gently loosen up the butter and mix in the sugar and salt. Then add the egg and vanilla extract
  2. Sift in the flour and mix. Refrigerate for about an hour.
Combine:
  1. Divide the white dough into 7g pieces and the Nutella dough into 10g pieces. Spread out the white Dough and wrap it around the Nutella dough.
  2. Preheat oven to 170°C.
  3. Place the dough balls on a backing tray and press them flat. Bake in the oven at 160°C for 12 minutes and let it cool.
  4. Enjoy!!
